# Vidrip Privacy Policy

_Last updated: [DATE]_

This Privacy Policy explains what information Vidrip ("Vidrip", "we", "us") collects, how we use it, and your choices. It applies to the Vidrip mobile app and vidrip.app.

> **Draft for review.** Have counsel review before publishing. Must be hosted at **https://www.vidrip.app/privacy** (the app links to this URL from the sign-up/sign-in agreement and from Account settings).

## Information we collect
- **Account info:** email, handle, display name, and (if used) invite code.
- **Content you create:** reaction videos, studio clips, comments, channel posts, messages, and their metadata (captions, timestamps, durations).
- **Camera & microphone:** captured only while you record. See "Camera & face effects" below.
- **Connected accounts (optional):** if you link a social account (e.g. YouTube), we access the data you authorize to power your feed.
- **Device & usage:** app interactions, diagnostics, and push tokens (for notifications).
- **Contacts (optional):** if you choose to invite friends, we read your contacts on-device to send invites. **Phone numbers are not uploaded or stored.**

## Camera & face effects
When you record a reaction, Vidrip uses your camera. If you choose an AR "lens," your device detects facial landmarks **on your device** using on-device machine learning. **Raw camera frames and facial geometry are not sent to our servers for face detection.** When a lens is used, a compact set of facial *landmark coordinates* (not images, not a faceprint) is saved with your video so the effect can be re-rendered on playback. We do **not** create a face template, do **not** use face data to identify you, and do **not** share it with third parties. Deleting your video removes this data.

_(When opt-in expression signals ship: If you opt in, Vidrip derives non-identifying gesture events (e.g. smiled, laughed) on your device to improve reaction features. We store only these events and summary scores — never your face, images, or any data that identifies you from your appearance. You can disable this anytime; the data is deleted with your videos and on account deletion.)_

## How we use information
- To provide the app: record, share, play, and moderate content; run your feed; deliver notifications.
- To keep Vidrip safe: detect and act on objectionable content and abusive behavior (see "Safety & moderation").
- To operate and improve the service and fix problems.

We do **not** sell your personal information. We do **not** use your face data for advertising or identification.

## Safety & moderation (user-generated content)
Vidrip hosts user-generated content. We provide tools to **report** content, **block** users, and we filter content (automated video screening and text filtering). When you block a user, their content is removed from your experience immediately and our moderation team is notified. We review reports and act on violations — including removing content and removing users — typically within 24 hours. There is no tolerance for objectionable content or abusive users.

## Sharing
We share information only with: service providers that run our infrastructure (e.g. cloud hosting/storage, push notifications, content-moderation processing of video frames), and as required by law. Providers are bound to use the data only to provide their service to us.

## Data retention & deletion
We keep content and account data while your account is active. You can delete individual content at any time, and you can delete your account, which removes your content and associated data (including any stored lens landmark data) from active systems. Backups are purged on a rolling schedule.

## Your choices & rights
- Manage permissions (camera, microphone, contacts, notifications) in your device settings.
- Delete content or your account in-app.
- Depending on your region (e.g. EEA/UK under GDPR, California under CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data, and to opt out of certain processing. Contact us to exercise these rights.

## Children
Vidrip is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children under that age.

## Changes
We may update this policy; we will post the new "Last updated" date and, for material changes, provide notice in the app.
